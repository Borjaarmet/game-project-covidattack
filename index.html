Coronavirus Attack

year 2021

Setup

The objective of the game is to try to catch 10 rolls of toilet paper, taking into account that the enemy (the virus) cannot touch you since it would infect you and you would die thus ending the game.For this you will have a little help and that is that you can shoot vaccines to end the virus.This way if you kill 10 viruses you can also win the game! The player can move to both sides, but keep in mind that if the player disappears on one side of the screen, he will appear directly on the other side.
